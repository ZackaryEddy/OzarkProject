History of the Country Store
The Country Store was built in 1943 by a small family of farmers. The building was initially used as a family house with an extra room for weary travelers on the Ozark River. By 1960, the family sold the building to a family traveling the river in search of a new homestead. The house was left in posession of the eldest son who decided to use the first floor as a trading post for people traveling the river. In 1968, he used his family money to add other buildings to the property.Then, in 1972 the Wild River Rapids company was opened to share his love of the river with as many people possible.

The Second Floor

The top floor of the country store now serves as a museum for the history of the river. Items ranging anywhere from arrow heads to fossilized aquatic life can be found in the museum. There are many fossils and other trinkets that can be purchased through the store. A video containing the history of the Ozark River and our company begins every hour on the hour. Also, there is a historian available at all times of operation hours to answer any questions and interests.
