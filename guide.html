Map and Directions

Getting to Wild River Rapids is easy - follow these detailed driving directions and don't listen to your GPS, just trust us (it doesn't understand these parts and takes you places you don't want to go).

  1. Go to Arkansas.
  2. Get on US-167 S/US-64 W/US-67 S in McRae Township from Copperas Springs Rd and AR-13 S.
  3. Follow US-167 S/US-67 S and I-530 S to US-425 S/US-65 S in Pine Bluff.
  4. Continue onto US-425 S/US-65 S.
  5. 9872 US-65.
  6. Follow Obvious sign that point to us, Wild River Rapids!


Wild River Rapids Routes and Destinations


Wild River Rapids offers a variety of river paths you can take. If you are looking for family friendly river routes, we recommend the tier 1 route. This route welcomes anyone between the ages of 12 and 60.

If you are interested in a more exiting experience, you can take our tier 2 route. Route 2 is more suitable for those between the ages of 18-60. We recommend taking a brief training session before taking route 2.

For the most intense experience, please look into our tier 3 route. There will be a more extensive training session before the excursion begins. Anyone who wishes to take the route 3, must be over 18 and must sign a safete waifer before embarking on this exiting route!

Please visit our rental page if you wish sign up for any of these routes.
